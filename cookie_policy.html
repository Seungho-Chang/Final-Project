[What are cookies?]

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. Cookies can help us to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences, the pages you visit, and the length of time you spend on our website. Cookies can also help us to analyze our website traffic and improve our website performance.

[What cookies do we use?]

We use Google Analytics to analyze our website traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, including the pages you visit and the length of time you spend on our site. This information is used to create reports about website traffic and to help us understand how visitors use our website.

[How to control cookies?]

You can control cookies by adjusting the settings on your browser. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. However, if you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the features of our website.
